About Us

Since Its Foundation in 1998, XRAY-LAB GmbH & Co. KG Has Developed into A Broadly Positioned International Company. What Began With The Business Area Of Industrial X-Ray Analysis in 2D and 3D Computer Tomography And Quality Analysis Was Quickly Expanded To Include Other Services In The Field Of NDT (Non-Destructive Testing Technology). Today, Well-known International Companies From The Automotive, Aerospace, Electronics And Defense Technology Sectors Are Among Our Customers.

Since Its Beginnings, The Company Has Made A Name For Itself in The Field Of X-Ray Examinations in 2D/2.5D And Computer Tomography. Today, We Can Offer Our Services, Machines And Software At Our Locations, Which Are Located in The Most Important Economic Regions Of The World.

We Provide Our Customers With A Machine Park With The Latest Technology From 60 KV To B MeV For Their Examinations. High Quantities And Series-Accompanying Tests Are Our Daily Work, As Is The Testing Of Individual Parts Or Components.

The Special Plant Construction Division in Combination With Our In-House Software Development Takes Care of Customer-Specific Machines For Inline Operation As Well As Special Machines And High-Energy Machines In The Research, Development And Laboratory Sectors. We Started Dealing With The Topic Of Artificial Intelligence (Al) Earty On And Developed Software For Evaluating Industrial X-Ray Images.
